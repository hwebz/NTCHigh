Who We Are

High Hotels Ltd., an affiliate of the High Real Estate Group LLC, develops, owns, and operates select-service and extended-stay hotels spanning four states in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast. Recognized as a leading operator, we're known for having maintained the highest standards in guest service and overall product quality for more than 20 years.

Our properties represent the best brands

We know successful hospitality begins with the right brand. For this reason we develop and operate only the hotel brands travelers prefer most, including Hampton Inn, Hilton Garden Inn, Homewood Suites by Hilton, Courtyard by Marriott, and Springhill Suites by Marriott.

Guest experience is backed by the best service

We do what it takes to create award-winning guest experience and hotels that rate among the best in their class. Our team takes great pride in having earned more than 100 awards for service, quality, and performance since our inception in 1988.

As one of the High® companies based in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, High Hotels is proud to be part of an organization with a long-standing tradition of building trustworthy relationships and providing quality products and services – since 1931.
