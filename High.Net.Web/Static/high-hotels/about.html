About Us

Home > About us

High Hotels Ltd. began in 1988 and is headquartered in Greenfield Corporate Center in Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The company is an affiliate of High Real Estate Group LCC and part of the High® companies — a multi-faceted group of businesses with a long-standing tradition for quality products and services since its founding more than 80 years ago.

Founded and based in Lancaster County Pennsylvania, the High organization's history began during the Great Depression in 1931 when Sanford High purchased King Welding Company. The company grew and expanded to include steel fabrication. Eventually, their expertise and technologi-cal leadership, rooted in welding, would be credited with revolutionizing bridge building.

The High organization's growth continued and is today a diverse organization that includes High Steel Structures, Inc.; High Concrete Group LLC; High Steel Service Center LLC; High Transit LLC; High Real Estate Group and its affiliates High Associates, Ltd.; High Construction Company; Greenfield Architects, Ltd.; High Investors, Ltd.; High Safety Consulting Services, Ltd.; and High Hotels.

The following is a snapshot of High Hotels' history :

1988 High Hotels Ltd. is started as an affiliated company of High Industries, Inc. to develop, acquire, and manage limited-service hotels in the Mid-Atlantic region.

1989 High Hotels' first hotel opens, Hampton Inn in Lancaster, PA

1990 Lancaster Hampton Inn wins first place Winegardner Award from Promus Hotels, Inc. as top performer in the system. Second hotel opens, Hampton Inn Harrisburg-West, in Mechanicsburg, PA.

1991 Hampton Inn - Harrisburg West wins first place Winegardner Award. Hampton Inn - Lancaster receives 2nd place.

1992 Third hotel opens, Hampton Inn, in York, PA.

1994 Fourth hotel and first acquisition for the company. Hampton Inn, in Reading, PA.

1995 Fifth hotel and first all-suites hotel product opens, Homewood Suites Harrisburg-West in Mechanicsburg, PA. This hotel also wins award from Promus Hotels, Inc. for having the 'Highest Guest Satisfaction Scores" in the history of Homewood Suites Hotels.

1997 Sixth hotel and first hotel outside of Pennsylvania opens, Hampton Inn & Suites in Annapolis, MD.

1999 Seventh hotel opens, Homewood Suites in Reading, PA. Eighth hotel opens, Hampton Inn, in Middletown, NY.

2001 32-Suite expansion of the Homewood Suites Harrisburg-West is completed.

2002 Ninth hotel and first Hilton Garden Inn opens in Wilkes-Barre, PA.

2003 Tenth hotel and first Courtyard by Marriott Hotel opens in Middletown, NY. Hampton Inn in Reading, PA, is sold.

2005 Eleventh hotel opens, Courtyard by Marriott, in Lancaster, PA.

2007 Homewood Suites Reading, PA, completes an expansion.

2010 Twelfth hotel and first SpringHill Suites Hotel opens in Ewing, NJ.

2012 Thirteenth hotel, Hampton Inn, Lexington Park, MD, is acquired.
