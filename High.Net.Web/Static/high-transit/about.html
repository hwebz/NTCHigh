Headquartered in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, High Transit LLC traces its roots back to the 1930's and the founding of the High Steel Structures Inc., the largest fabricator of structural steel in North America.

Formally incorporated in 2006, High Transit supplies the shipping fleet functions for High Steel Structures Inc., High Concrete Group LLC, and High Steel Service Center LLC. Additionally, High Transit partners with other heavy hauling companies to provide a wide range of turn-key heavy hauling services.

Our experienced staff and professional drivers are ready to assist in the transport of especially long or heavy loads.

Our service offerings include in-house engineering for load stress analysis, routing analysis from origin to destination, permit ordering, escort trucks and drivers, certified mechanics, and management of each move, providing updates on load movement, progress, and timing of arrival.

We invite you to learn more about High Transit's recent projects, history, focus on safety, industry affiliations, and guiding philosophy.
