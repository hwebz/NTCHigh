ABOUT US

StructureCare is the one-stop for preserving, maintaining, repairing and restoring parking structures, including emergency repairs, long-term maintenance plans, and conditions assessments. The StructureCare team provides a full-service balance of engineering consulting, and restoration, repair and maintenance services. The end result for all our customers is solutions, developed through a trusted relationship between our experts and you, the customer.

A service and consulting resource focused on developing and implementing preventive maintenance programs for garages of all construction types, we provide comprehensive services to a growing number of institutional owners throughout the mid-Atlantic and Northeast. Our unique engineering approach helps extend the life of your parking garage through:

Detailed Condition Assessments — which include documentation of current conditions, root cause analysis of failures, prioritized repair recommendations along with order of magnitude pricing.

Preventive Maintenance Program Development — provides an organized, site specific operations and maintenance plan.

In-Service Training of Facility Staff — trains staff to implement portions of the PM plan, including necessary periodic and winter maintenance.

Restoration Management and Implementation — our engineering and field teams can assist in designing restoration strategies and, if desired, per-form necessary repairs.

Structural Preventive Maintenance Services — experienced field personnel can perform routine maintenance services beyond the scope of facility staff.

The StructureCare program extends the extensive engineering and field resource of High Concrete Group LLC to garage owners to help preserve and manage their parking assets at the lowest possible operating cost. For more information on StructureCare® call 1-800-PRECAST, use our contact form or email fgenoese@high.net.
