Commercial Office

Greenfield Architects provides architecture and design services for office buildings in PA, NY, NJ, DE, MD, and surrounding states. We design a variety of facilities from large corporate headquarter buildings to small commercial offices.

Programming space for maximum efficiency and effectiveness, addressing growth concerns, boosting employee morale, and creating a building that enhances your company's image are your top concerns—and ours.
