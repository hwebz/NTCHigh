Welcome

Guided by The High Philosophy principle of building trustworthy relationships, High Real Estate Group and its affiliates are dedicated to exceeding the expectations of each of our clients as we provide solutions to their real estate- and construction-related challenges.

Our portfolio of office, industrial, multi-family residential, retail, and hotel properties exceeds 10 million square feet and spans the eastern u.S. from New York to Florida. These properties — each managed by our own teams of professionals — provide our commercial/industrial tenants, hotel guests and apartment community residents with high-value, well-maintained facilities backed by responsive service from a company that truly cares about customer satisfaction.

And through our affiliates. — High Associates Ltd., High construction company, GreenField Architects Ltd., High Hotels Ltd.. and High Investors Ltd. — we provide development, brokerage, property management and tenant care services, architecture and construction services. appraisal services. hospitality services, and real estate investment opportunities.

Our goal is to earn your confidence and trust by providing the exact combination of real estate services needed to most effectively achieve your business goals. we look forward to the opportunity to be of service to you.
