Bridge Projects

The new 9 Mile Bridge was opened within 65 days of notice to proceed.

9 Mile Road Bridge Hazel Park, MI

High Steel fabricated and shipped 317 tons of structural steel for the design-build replacement of the 9 Mile Road bridge at I-75 in Hazel Park, MI. The first shipment of steel arrived at the jobsite just 21 days after notice to proceed.

The finished Arthur Ravenel Jr. bridge shown at night.

Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge over the Cooper River
Charleston - Mt. Pleasant, SC

Charleston's landmark Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge over the Cooper River carries with it some impressive statistics that help explain why it was High Steel's largest job ever. The 3.5-mile, eight-lane span of graceful white steel is the longest cable-stay bridge in North America.

Brightman Street Bridge Replacement
Fall River - Somerset, MA

High Steel fabricated and erected the CSX railroad bridges over MD450 and Upshur Street

Batchellerville Bridge, Edinburg, New York

High Steel fabricated and erected four massive railroad girders for the Maryland State Highway Administration’s $70 million railroad bridge construction project on MD 450 and Upshur Street in Prince George’s County. The project required a total of 749 tons of steel.
