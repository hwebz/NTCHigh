Privacy Policy

Premier Self Storage and the High® companies believe strongly in protecting the privacy of the personally identifiable information you share with us, and respecting the permission you give us to use that information. This Privacy Statement notifies you of our privacy practices and describes how we will use your information, and describes the choices you have concerning how your information is collected and used. This will make it easier for you to:

Request information from Premier Self Storage and the High companies specific to your interests

Receive personalized messages to your specific questions

Save time by storing your preferences

It will also allow us to conduct market research to help us improve our products, our services and our Web site.

We will only collect, use or share your personally identifiable information if you give us explicit permission to do so. By completing your application form or sending us E-mail, you are giving us permission to use and share your information. You should know that by your application form or sending us E-mail you are giving Premier Self Storage permission to transfer your personally identifiable information between the High® companies to fulfill your requirement. However, we will not share any information you provide with other companies.

In general, you can visit Premier Self Storage without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. Our Web servers collect domain E-mail addresses of visitors. This information is aggregated to measure the average time spent on the site, and pages viewed. High uses this information to see the use of our site and improve the content of our site.
