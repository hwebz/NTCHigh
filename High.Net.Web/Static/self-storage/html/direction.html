Driving Directions

From U.S. Route 30 East:

Take the Greenfield Road exit. Turn left at the traffic light onto Greenfield Road. Turn left (just before the railroad tracks) onto Independence Court. Follow the road to Premier Self Storage at the end of Independence Court.

From U.S. Route 30 West:

Take the Greenfield Road exit. Turn left at the traffic light onto Greenfield Road. Go straight at the next traffic light, then turn left (just before the railroad tracks) onto Independence Court. Follow the road to Premier Self Storage at the end of Independence Court.
