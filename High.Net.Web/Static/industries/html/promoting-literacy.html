Home > About Us > Community Commitment > Promoting Literacy

When staying at a Hampton Inn®, Homewood Suites by Hilton®, Hilton Garden Inn®, or Courtyard by Marriott, each guest of High Hotels Ltd. deserves and receives award-winning service. As vice president of marketing and operations at High Hotels, Stephanie Reese and the team of more than 400 co-workers never take guest satisfaction for granted. And Stephanie does not take reading for granted either. As a volunteer tutor with the Literacy Council of Lancaster-Lebanon, she teaches adults to read—opening doors and creating new opportunities for her students as they move beyond the restrictive effects of illiteracy. Stephanie Reese and the Literacy Council of Lancaster-Lebanon are making a difference in our community.
