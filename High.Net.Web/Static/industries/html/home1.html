High industries affiliates serve diverse customers throughout the Eastern and Midwestern United states. our goals are to provide the best possible value and to lead in our markets through service and innovation.

High Steel Structures has won a reputation for bridge steel of superior quality and fit in the North American transportation industry. High Concrete Group, a leading producer of architectural and structural precast products for building, is frequently chosen to handle large and complex projects. New investments position High Steel Service Center to be a key supplier of flat panel, stress free metal products.

Our newest affiliates, High Transit and High structural Erectors. are examples of market innovation, growing from departments with existing businesses to become the specialty hauler and erector for High Industries and for industry at large.

All High Industries affiliates share a dedication to responsible stewardship of the environment and the greater community. Together with our commitment to co-worker health and to sustainable growth, we are strengthening our capabilities and deepening our roots to be your business partner of choice.
