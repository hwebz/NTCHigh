Corporate Leadership and Governance

Michael F. Shirk, Chief Executive Officer of the High companies

Michael F. Shirk oversees High Real Estate Group LLC; High Industries Inc.; and High Company LLC, High’s business support functions. In addition, he oversees High Real Estate Group affiliates High Construction Company and Greenfield Architects Ltd. Other High Real Estate Group affiliates are High Associates Ltd., High Hotels Ltd., High Safety Consulting Services Ltd., and High Investors Ltd. High Industries affiliates are High Steel Structures LLC, High Concrete Group LLC, High Steel Service Center LLC, High Structural Erectors LLC, and High Transit LLC.
