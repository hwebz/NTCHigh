Industrial/Commercial Real Estate Brokerage Services

High Associates' brokerage team provides marketing expertise in selling and leasing industrial and commercial real estate — including warehouse, office and retail space — as well as buyer and tenant representation. Our offices are located in Lancaster, Harrisburg, and Coatesville, Pa.: Charlotte,N.C.; and Sarasota, Fla. Our real estate professionals are knowledgeable and experienced Realtors ready to assist you in achieving your real estate objectives.
