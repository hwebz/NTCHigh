Apartment Communities

High Associates manages apartment communities featuring lovely townhomes and garden apartments for rent in Lancaster and Pottstown, PA; Charlotte, NC; and Summerville and Charleston, SC.

With more than 30 years of experience, our award-winning management team is dedicated to exceeding our residents' expectations. Our apartment communities are maintained to the highest standards, and our on-site staff is committed to providing exceptional resident service.

We invite you to explore our apartment community websites, which provide information specific to each community and offer the convenience of online rent payment and service requests through our Access 24/7 system. Please contact our on-site representatives via the apartment community website links on this page to learn how you can make one of our beautiful apartments your new home.
