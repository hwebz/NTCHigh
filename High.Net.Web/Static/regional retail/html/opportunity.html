The Crossings at Conestoga Creek

A New-Generation Mixed Use Development Shop, Dine, Live, and Stay
The Crossings at Conestoga Creek is a planned Main Street-oriented mixed-use development that will combine retail, hospitality, and multi-family residential components in a fun, pedestrian-friendly, open-air design.
