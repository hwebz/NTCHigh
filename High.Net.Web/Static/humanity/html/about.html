ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The Essence of Humanity Award honors those who inspire the best in all of us, through the giving of the award itself and also by elevating the stories of these role models in our communities. The award is presented by participating organizations in collaboration with the Essence of Humanity program’s steering committee, which evaluates the nominations. For more information, contact Robin Stauffer, Executive Director, at 717-293-4498 or RStauffer@high.net.

Award criteria

The Essence of Humanity Award is presented to

1. Individuals who have overcome challenges through adversity or obstacles involving economic hardship, physical disabilities, physical and/or mental illness, etc.

and

2. Caregivers who have exhibited the values we seek to recognize in delivering special care for those individuals described in item 1 above. These individuals may be a parent, extended family member, coach, teacher, mentor, guidance counselor, neighbor, benefactor, etc.

The selection of the Award winner in each of the categories above will be made by a Steering Committee in the local area, based on the criteria cited. Each nomination will be considered on its own merit and in relation to other nominations submitted.

Values we seek to recognize

The values this program has established as those it seeks to recognize include, but are not limited to:

  • Courage
  • Love
  • Strength
  • Determination
  • Encouragement
  • Integrity
  • Inspiration
  • Persistence
  • Support
  • Gratitude
  • Optimism
  • Faith
  • Hope
